# OctoAcme Project Management Docs

Welcome to the OctoAcme project management documentation! This repository centralizes our key project delivery processes to enable repeatable, high-quality execution.

## Overview of Project Management Processes

OctoAcme employs a structured project management approach centered on iterative delivery and customer-first principles. The core workflow follows a lifecycle of initiation, planning, execution, release, and retrospective, ensuring projects deliver value incrementally while maintaining clear ownership through dedicated roles. Key artifacts such as project charters, roadmaps, backlogs, and risk registers support this process, with data-informed decisions driving continuous improvement.

Key personas include Project Managers who coordinate delivery and manage risks, Product Managers who define outcomes and prioritize backlogs, Developers who implement features with quality in mind, QA/Testing specialists who validate acceptance criteria, and Stakeholders who provide inputs and approvals. This clear delineation of responsibilities fosters accountability and efficient collaboration across cross-functional teams.

Communication is facilitated through regular cadences such as weekly syncs between PM and PdM, twice-weekly standups for delivery teams, and monthly stakeholder updates. Risk management involves maintaining a risk register with assessments of impact and likelihood, while escalations follow defined paths for timely resolution. Incident communication templates ensure transparency during issues.

Quality assurance is integral, with practices including unit and integration testing, end-to-end smoke tests, security scanning in CI, and manual QA for feature acceptance. Retrospectives capture learnings to drive continuous improvement, and deployments follow standardized checklists to minimize risks and ensure smooth releases.
