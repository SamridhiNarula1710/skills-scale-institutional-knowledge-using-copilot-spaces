# OctoAcme — Risk Assessment Template

## Purpose
This template provides a structured approach to assess and document project risks, ensuring consistent evaluation and mitigation planning.

## Risk Assessment Criteria
- **Impact**: High, Medium, Low (potential effect on project timeline, budget, quality, or scope)
- **Likelihood**: High, Medium, Low (probability of occurrence)
- **Priority**: Calculated as combination of Impact and Likelihood (e.g., High Impact + High Likelihood = Critical)

## Usage Notes
- Complete this template for each identified risk during project planning and execution.
- Update regularly during weekly syncs.
- Escalate critical risks immediately to project sponsor.
- Use this template to maintain the Risk Register.
